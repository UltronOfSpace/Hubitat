# Example Apps for PauseResumeLib

Alright boys, welcome to the greasy Example Apps for PauseResumeLib! These here examples show you how to use the [PauseResumeLib](../README.md) library to make your Hubitat apps even more decent. I whipped up a couple of examples to get you started, whether you're runnin' a big boss app with a bunch of little punk kid apps or just a lone wolf app that don't need nobody.

## What's in Here

- [**ParentAndChild**](ParentAndChild/README.md): This example got a big boss app (`PauseResumeLib Parent App Example`) that makes a bunch of little punk kid apps and tells 'em what to do, plus the little punk kid app (`PauseResumeLib Child App Example`) that listens to the big boss. Perfect for when you're managin' a whole crew of apps.
- [**StandAlone**](StandAlone/README.md): This example's a lone wolf app that don't need nobody—just its own pause button to chill out whenever you want. Great for a single app that's doin' its own thing.

## How to Use These Examples

Each example got its own README with all the deets on how to get 'em installed and runnin' on your Hubitat setup. You'll need to install the PauseResumeLib library first—check out the [PauseResumeLib README](../README.md) for how to do that. Then, head into the example's folder, follow the steps in its README, and you'll be good to go quicker than you can say "decent"!
